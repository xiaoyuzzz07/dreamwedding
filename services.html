DINING

We offer couples a gourmet selection of Chinese, Malay, or Western cuisine, expertly crafted by our award-winning master chefs. For larger weddings, a food tasting for up to ten guests is included. We also provide a complimentary five-tier decorative wedding cake, along with individually wrapped macarons or pralines as wedding favors, personalised to your chosen colours and flavours.

PERFECT TOUCHES

To complement our beautiful venues, we can arrange a number of wonderful touches. We have excellent relationships with external suppliers and will happily put you in touch with recommended photographers, decorators and entertainers.

PAMPER YOURSELF

Allow us to pamper you as you prepare for your big day by visiting our relaxing spa. We offer a range of therapies, as well as access to our sauna, steam room, and whirlpool, ensuring you remain stress-free on your special day. We also have a hair and beauty salon, where our stylists will help you look your very best.

WEDDING PLANNING

We are dedicated to helping you plan your dream wedding for a truly memorable day. Our pillar-less Grand Ballroom, exceptional service and exquisite catering will deliver the ultimate wedding experience. Work with our expert wedding specialists to design a celebration that reflects your personal style and fulfils your dreams.
