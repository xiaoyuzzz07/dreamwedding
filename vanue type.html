Crystal Room

Designed for intimate elegance, the Crystal Room offers a warm and exclusive atmosphere perfect for close-knit celebrations of up to 100 guests. The space is beautifully appointed with classic gold Chiavari chairs and soft table linens, centered around tall, romantic floral arrangements that add height and sophistication to the dining experience.

Grand Ballroom

The expansive Grand Ballroom creates a majestic setting for large-scale weddings, capable of hosting up to 1,100 guests in absolute luxury. Featuring a spectacular stage framed by opulent gold arches and lush illuminated floral displays, this versatile space can be divided or used in its entirety to deliver a truly royal experience for the bride and groom.

Diamond Ballroom

Situated on the ground floor with its own private entrance, the Diamond Ballroom offers a romantic and accessible venue for up to 300 guests. The room is defined by its soft, shimmering drapery and a pristine white aisle that leads to a floral-adorned stage, providing a flexible layout that is perfect for capturing memorable photo opportunities.
